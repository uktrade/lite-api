Commercial exploitation agreements

If the item is designed by or for the MOD, you must have a commercial exploitation agreement before it can be manufactured or sold commercially. This is the case regardless of whether MOD owns the commercial design right.

Get help with your commercial exploitation agreement by contacting:

  • IPRG, Poplar 2, MOD, Abbey Wood, Bristol BS34 8JH if the MOD owns intellectual property rights relating to the equipment
  • the MOD Contracts Branch who issued the original contract if the company owns the design rights (but MOD can claim a levy)
